A dead simple website for saving the things you want to read.
  1. What data is collected

    The Lucentsave Chrome extension collects and uses the following data:

    • Authentication JWT token: This is collected when you log into the Lucentsave extension. This token is saved in the cookies of Lucentsave.com and is used to authenticate your login session.
    • Saved pages: The extension collects the HTML of the page you're currently reading when you choose to save it through the extension. This is used to save the page's reader-viewed HTML on our backend.
    • User emails and hashed passwords: Collected when you create an account or log in. These are used for account creation and authentication.
    • Saved posts and highlights: Collected when you save a post or create a highlight. These are used to provide our service to you, allowing you to save, read, and organize pages.
  2. How the data is used

    The collected data is used to provide and improve our services. We do not share your data with third parties, nor do we use it for personalized advertisements, credit-worthiness assessments, or lending purposes. The collected data is only used in ways that are necessary to provide or improve the single purpose of the Lucentsave extension.

  3. User Consent

    By using the Lucentsave Chrome extension, you consent to this Privacy Policy and agree to the data collection and usage outlined herein. We only collect data after you've given your consent and we ensure that we only collect data that is necessary to provide our services.

  4. Security Measures

    We take your data security seriously. We employ industry standard security measures to protect your data and we review and update our security measures regularly.

  5. Changes to this Policy

    This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The updated policy will be posted on this page, so please review it periodically.
